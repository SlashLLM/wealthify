Fund Types

Find your fund

Your fund type is the single biggest lever in your KiwiSaver. Most people have never changed it.

Conservative
~3–4% p.a. net
Low risk High risk
Mostly cash and bonds. Low volatility but low growth. Suitable when retirement is very close — within 2–3 years.
Best for: Retiring soon
Balanced
~5–6% p.a. net
Low risk High risk
Mix of shares and bonds. Moderate growth with some volatility buffer. Good middle ground for those 10+ years from retirement.
Best for: 10–15 yrs to retire
MOST POPULAR
Growth
~7–8% p.a. net
Low risk High risk
Mostly shares — global and NZ equities. Stronger long-term returns with short-term market swings. Best choice for most working Kiwis.
Best for: 15+ yrs to retire
Aggressive
~8–10% p.a. net
Low risk High risk
100% high-growth equities. Maximum long-term potential but significant swings. Stomach for volatility required. Best for young investors.
Best for: 20+ yrs, high risk
